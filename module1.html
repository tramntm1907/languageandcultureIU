Module 1

Language, Culture, and Identity

This module introduces the relationship between language, cultural meaning, and identity formation.

Key Ideas

  • Language carries cultural memory.
  • Identity is performed through language choices.
  • Multilingual speakers may negotiate several identities.

Mini Reading

Language is not only a neutral communication system. It is a social resource through which people position themselves and others. Accent, vocabulary, politeness style, and code-switching may all signal belonging, distance, authority, or solidarity.

Reflection Task

Write 150 words about one language variety that reflects your personal, family, regional, or academic identity.
